# Pricing Strategy for Yu-Gi-Oh! Game

## Pricing Tiers
1. **Free Tier**: Limited access to basic simulations and tutorials.
2. **Monthly Subscription**: $9.99/month for full access to all content and features.
3. **Annual Subscription**: $99.99/year, offering a 20% discount compared to the monthly plan.

## Rationale
- **Free Tier**: Lowers the barrier to entry, allowing users to experience the app and increasing conversion to paid tiers.
- **Monthly Subscription**: Provides flexibility for users who prefer short-term commitment.
- **Annual Subscription**: Encourages long-term commitment with a discount, increasing customer lifetime value.

## Willingness-to-Pay
- Based on surveys and competitor analysis, returning players are willing to pay for a specialized learning tool that enhances their skills and incorporates nostalgia.
- Pricing is competitive with other subscription-based mobile games and educational apps, balancing value and affordability.
