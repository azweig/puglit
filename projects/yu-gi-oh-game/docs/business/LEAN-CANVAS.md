# Lean Canvas for Yu-Gi-Oh! Game

## Problem
1. Returning players struggle to keep up with modern Yu-Gi-Oh! gameplay mechanics and strategies.
2. Lack of structured learning tools for mastering advanced duel tactics like breaking boards and finding lethal lines.
3. Existing apps focus more on gameplay than on teaching and skill development.

## Solution
- Develop an app with guided mini-duel simulations that teach real duel decisions, focusing on breaking boards and finding lethal lines.
- Provide interactive tutorials and feedback to enhance learning and retention.

## Unique Value Proposition
- "Rediscover the thrill of Yu-Gi-Oh! with modern strategies and become a master duelist through our guided learning app."

## Unfair Advantage
- Exclusive partnerships with Yu-Gi-Oh! brand and content creators for unique content and promotions.
- Advanced AI-driven simulations that personalize learning experiences.

## Customer Segments
1. Returning Yu-Gi-Oh! players seeking to improve their skills.
2. New players interested in learning advanced strategies.
3. Competitive players looking to enhance their duel tactics.

## Channels
- Mobile app stores (iOS and Android)
- Social media advertising (Facebook, Instagram, YouTube)
- Partnerships with Yu-Gi-Oh! influencers and content creators

## Cost Structure
- Development and maintenance of the app
- Licensing fees for Yu-Gi-Oh! content
- Marketing and advertising expenses
- Customer support and community management

## Revenue Streams
- Subscription model with monthly and annual plans
- In-app purchases for additional content and features

## Key Metrics
- Monthly Active Users (MAU)
- Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)
- Lifetime Value (LTV)
- Churn Rate
- Engagement rates on simulations and tutorials
