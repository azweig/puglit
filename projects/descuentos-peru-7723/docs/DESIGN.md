# Design brief — Descuentos Perú

**Descuentos Perú Visual Identity Design Brief**

**MOOD**
The design for Descuentos Perú should evoke a sense of excitement and discovery, much like uncovering hidden treasures in your everyday routine. Think about the thrill of a treasure hunt combined with the convenience of modern technology. Reference the vibrant, engaging aesthetics of apps like Airbnb for exploration and TripAdvisor for local discovery.

**COLOR ROLES**
- **Background**: bg-[#581845] — A deep, rich color that sets a luxurious yet approachable base.
- **Surface/Cards**: bg-[#FFFFFF] — Clean and clear, ensuring content stands out.
- **Primary CTA**: bg-[#FF5733] — A bright, inviting color encouraging interaction.
- **Accent**: bg-[#900C3F] — Used sparingly for emphasis and highlights.
- **Text**: text-[#FFFFFF] — Crisp and readable against darker backgrounds.
- **Muted**: text-[#C70039] — For secondary information, providing contrast without overpowering.

**TYPE**
Typography should convey confidence and clarity. Use bold, large headings to grab attention (e.g., font-bold text-2xl), paired with medium-weight body text (e.g., font-medium text-base) for readability. Ensure titles are prominent and engaging.

**COMPONENT RECIPES**
- **Cards**: Use rounded-3xl, shadow-lg, and bg-[#FFFFFF] with text-[#581845] for content. Cards should feel tactile and inviting.
- **Primary Buttons**: rounded-full, bg-[#FF5733], text-[#FFFFFF], px-4 py-2, hover:bg-[#C70039].
- **Secondary Buttons**: rounded-full, border border-[#FF5733], text-[#FF5733], px-4 py-2, hover:bg-[#FF5733] hover:text-[#FFFFFF].
- **Chips/Badges**: rounded-full, bg-[#900C3F], text-[#FFFFFF], px-3 py-1 for status indicators.
- **Inputs**: rounded-md, border border-[#C70039], bg-[#FFFFFF], text-[#581845], px-3 py-2, focus:border-[#FF5733].
- **Bottom Tab Bar**: bg-[#581845], text-[#FFFFFF], flex justify-around, py-2, with active text-[#FF5733].

**MOTION**
Incorporate subtle transitions such as transition-all duration-200 ease-in-out for hover effects on buttons and cards. Use transform scale-105 for a gentle zoom effect on card hover.

**PER-SCREEN LAYOUT**
- **/app (Descubrir)**: Full-bleed photo card stack with gradient scrim overlay (bg-gradient-to-t from-black to-transparent), large circular like/pass buttons (rounded-full, bg-[#FF5733]), and title/price text-large, bold overlaid.
- **/app/programs (Mis Programas)**: Grid layout of loyalty programs with interactive cards.
- **/app/location (Ubicación)**: Map-centric view with pins for nearby discounts, list of locations below.
- **/app/merchants (Merchants)**: List view with merchant cards, featuring logo and current offers.
- **/app/branches (Branches)**: Grid or list of branches with quick-access info.
- **/app/offers (Offers)**: Marketplace-style grid, each item with a prominent image, offer details, and CTA.

This identity should be consistently applied across all screens to ensure a seamless and engaging user experience.
